Expert Apace Kafka & Confluent Training

Confluent offers training for both operators and developers using Kafka, through the following following courses developed by our team of Kafka experts. For more information regarding these courses.

OPS-101 Kafka Operations AND Internals

What you'll learn:

At the end of this course you will be prepared to build and manage Kafka clusters using industry best practices developed by the world’s foremost Kafka experts. During this course you’ll learn about the main subsystems of Kafka, their functions, how they interact, and their set-up, operation, and tuning. We’ll also study critical technical knowledge such as the lifecycles of reads and writes, how data is stored on disk, the Kafka protocol, log compaction, use of command-line tools, and how to diagnose latency and throughput issues.

DEV-101 Building Kafka Solutions

What you'll learn:

At the end of this course you will be prepared to build an application that can publish data to, and subscribe to data from, a Kafka cluster. During this course, a Kafka expert from Confluent will engage with your team to review the role of Kafka in the modern data distribution pipeline, discuss core Kafka architectural concepts and components, and review the Kafka developer APIs. We’ll also discuss the other components of the broader Confluent Platform, the specific business needs your application addresses, and the best approach to leveraging Kafka to meet those needs.
